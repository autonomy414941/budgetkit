FAQ

What emergency fund size should I use?

Start with 3 months of essentials, then move toward 6+ if income is variable or single-source.

Can I use this for debt payoff planning?

Yes. Add your extra debt payment inside the savings/debt field in the monthly budget section.

Why are my 50/30/20 numbers hard to match?

High housing costs often push needs above 50%. Use the split as a target, then iterate gradually.
