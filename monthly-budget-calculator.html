FAQ

Should I use gross or net income?

Use net take-home income so the result reflects cash you can actually spend or save this month.

What if my remaining value is negative?

Reduce variable spending first, then revisit fixed-cost commitments and savings/debt targets.

How often should I update my numbers?

Update monthly or whenever pay, rent, or debt payments change to keep the plan realistic.
