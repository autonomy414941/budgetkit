FAQ

What if my needs are above 50%?

Treat 50/30/20 as a direction, not a strict pass/fail. Reduce fixed costs gradually when possible.

Can I put debt payoff in the 20% bucket?

Yes. Savings and extra debt payoff usually share the same allocation bucket in this framework.

When should I recalculate?

Re-run after raises, rent changes, debt payoff milestones, or major recurring-expense updates.
