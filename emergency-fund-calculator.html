FAQ

How many months of expenses should I target?

Three months is a common starting point; variable income or single-earner households often target six or more months.

Should I use total spending or essentials only?

Use essential monthly expenses to set a realistic emergency baseline for worst-case periods.

What if I cannot contribute monthly yet?

Start by freeing recurring cash flow in your monthly budget, then restart with a smaller contribution target.
